Our Guiding
Principles

Focused Expertise in Dispute Resolution

Disputes are our core practice. We handle arbitration and commercial litigation across forums, providing clear, practical, and effective advice. We take on matters where we can add real value, approaching each case with straightforward insight.

Tailored Dispute Solutions

We believe that there is no one-size-fits-all approach. Our team works closely with clients to identify potential dispute prevention options and devise mechanisms to identify friction points, develop negotiation and settlement strategies and document protective measures, such as settlement agreements, tailored contractual clauses and dispute-resolution protocols to safeguard commercial relationships while protecting legal interests.

Sector-Specific and Cross-Border Expertise

We bring specialised insight into sectors such as infrastructure, energy, construction, financial services, private equity and technology. Our experience across diverse industries enables us to handle complex cross-border disputes with the nuance they demand. We also assist multinational clients in navigating India’s arbitration and litigation landscape, ensuring seamless coordination across jurisdictions.

Cohesive Expert Management

Acting as a single point of contact, we coordinate all external and internal experts, including barristers/counsel for advocacy, asset managers, forensic experts and valuation specialists for discovery and quantum analysis. We also work with leading third-party funders to ensure consistent strategy, smooth communication, and effective execution.

Boutique Precision, Big Law Rigor

Our team combines the intellectual rigor and procedural expertise of top-tier law firms with the agility, attention, and personal involvement of a boutique practice. We take pride in our written advocacy and conduct our own oral advocacy in arbitrations in relevant cases. In complex and large-scale arbitrations or cross border litigation, we involve the co-counsel from leading international firms, senior advocates/barristers or subject-matter specialists to deliver results and strategies tailored to our client’s unique needs.

AI-Enhanced Insights

We integrate AI and advanced tools with human expertise to strengthen legal research, data analysis and case strategy. This helps us improve efficiency in case strategy and enable faster and more precise decisions.
